Hornan Tonic

ingredients

  • Lime Zest
  • Lime Juice
  • Elderflower liquor
  • Gin
  • Tonic

Experience the Nordic Breeze with Hornan tonic - a delightful blend of zesty lime, crisp gin, bubbly tonic, and a touch of enchanting elderflower liquor. This drink encapsulates the essence of Scandinavia’s awe-inspiring nature with every sip. The lime brings a refreshing forest breeze-like zing, while the gin’s botanical notes transport you to a lush meadow. The bubbles of tonic mimic sparkling lakes, and the subtle sweetness of elderflower liquor dances through like wildflowers in a field. Toast to the Scandinavian wilderness with a drink that’s as refreshing as a stroll through the woods!
