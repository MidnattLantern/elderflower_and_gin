Gessi Tonic

ingredients

  • Lime Zest
  • Lime Juice
  • Elderflower Juice
  • Tonic
Looking for a refreshing and non-alcoholic drink option? Why not try a delicious cocktail made with lime, tonic, and elderflower juice! This drink is perfect for those looking for a light and refreshing beverage that is both tasty and easy to make. Simply mix together the ingredients and serve over ice for a truly refreshing treat. With its zesty lime flavor and sweet elderflower notes, this cocktail is sure to become a favorite among your friends and family. So why not give it a try today and see for yourself just how delicious it can be!
