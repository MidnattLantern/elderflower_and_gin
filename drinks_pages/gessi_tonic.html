Gessi Tonic

ingredients

  • Lime Zest
  • Lime Juice
  • Elderflower Cider (non-alcoholic)
  • Tonic

Looking for a refreshing and non-alcoholic drink option? Why not try a delicious cocktail made with lime, tonic, and elderflower juice? This drink is perfect for those looking for a light and refreshing beverage that is both tasty and easy to make. Simply mix together the ingredients and serve over ice for a truly refreshing treat. With its zesty lime flavour and sweet elderflower notes, this cocktail is sure to become a favourite among your friends and family.
