Hornan Tonic

ingredients

  • Gin
  • Tonic
  • Lime Zest
  • Lime Juice
  • Elderflower Liquor

Taste the Nordic Breeze. Hornan tonic is a delightful mix of zesty lime, crisp gin, bubbly tonic, and a touch of enchanting elderflower liquor. This drink captures the essence of Scandinavia's breathtaking nature with every sip. The lime adds a zing-like refreshing forest breeze, while the gin's botanical notes transport you to lush meadows. Bubbles of tonic mimic the sparkling lakes, and the subtle sweetness of elderflower liquor dances through like wildflowers in the fields. Raise your glass to the Scandinavian wilderness in a drink that's as invigorating as a walk through the woods!
